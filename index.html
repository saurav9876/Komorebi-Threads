Our Story

Komorebi Threads was born from a deep appreciation for India's rich natural landscapes and cultural heritage. We believe clothing can be both functional and magical – items that transport you to misty mountains, lush forests, and tranquil backwaters while being comfortable and sustainable.

Each piece is crafted with care using eco-friendly materials and ethical production methods by skilled artisans across India. We draw inspiration from the small wonders – the dance of light through banyan trees, the rustle of wind in rice fields, and the quiet moments of beauty in daily life throughout our diverse country.
